Privacy Policy

This privacy policy describes how your personal information is collected and used when you access and use the website https://www.bothahome.co.za.

Information Collection and Use

This website uses Google OAuth for authentication purposes. When you log in using your Google account, we may receive your name and email address as provided by Google. This information is solely used to grant you access to the website.

We do not collect any other personal information when you visit this website.

Data Retention

The website retains your login information only for the duration of your active session. Once you log out, this information is no longer actively stored by the website.

Sharing of Information

We do not share your personal information with any third parties.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information collected through Google OAuth. However, please understand that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is completely secure.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us through the information provided on the website.
