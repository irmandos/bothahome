Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 28, 2026

This privacy policy describes how your personal information is managed when you access and use the website bothahome.co.za.

1. No Personal Data Collection

BothaHome is a purely static, informational landing page presenting the online resumes and professional portfolios of members of the Botha family. We do not collect, request, store, or process any personal information from our visitors. There are no registration forms, contact forms, or newsletter subscriptions hosted on this website.

2. No Authentication or Third-Party Logins

This website does not require user accounts. We do not use Google OAuth, Facebook Login, or any other authentication methods. Any past references to Google OAuth in previous documentation have been deprecated, as this website is entirely public and free of logins.

3. Third-Party Portfolios & Links

This website contains links to external sites (such as LinkedIn profiles and personal developer portfolios like irmandos.co.za and anuscke.tech). We do not control and are not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or cookie policies of these external platforms. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of any site you visit.

4. Analytics

To understand general site traffic without invading your privacy, we utilize Cloudflare Web Analytics. This analytics tool is privacy-focused:

  • It does not use cookies.
  • It does not track you across different websites.
  • It does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII).

5. Compliance with POPIA & GDPR

Because we do not collect, store, or process personal information, our website natively aligns with the high standards set by the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) of South Africa, as well as the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) of the European Union.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted directly on this page with an updated effective date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can reach out to us using the contact details or portfolio pages linked on the main landing page.
